# Privacy Policy for sTabControl

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Data Collection
sTabControl does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Local Storage
The extension stores your preferences (tab position, close behavior, theme) using Chrome's sync storage API. This data:
- Never leaves your browser/Google account
- Is only used to remember your settings
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions Explained
- `tabs`: Reorder and focus tabs
- `storage`: Save your preferences
- `webNavigation`: Detect duplicate URLs

## Third Parties
No third-party services, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
