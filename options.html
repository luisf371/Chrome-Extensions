Options

Appearance

Scanning

Time to wait for a site to respond. Default: 5s.
Lower values scan faster but may report false positives for slow sites.
Recommended: 3s - 10s.

Sorting

If checked, bookmarks will be sorted immediately when a scan starts.
"Parent Folders Only" sorts just the folders you see in the bar/menu structure.
"Recursive" sorts all folders and all bookmarks within them.
